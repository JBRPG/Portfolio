Problem

Data mangement is a field and skill that is intensely associated with computer science. However, not too many data mangement tools are designed with end users in mind.

I have been assigned to the Local Ground team of Berkely for Cornell University's 'From Data to Awesome' with the following task: How to manage data at browser level?

To best tackle the problem, the team and I had to take the following issues that had to be addressed:

  • Visualize data
  • Designed for complete beginners
  • Simply add, edit, and delete data

Goal

Even though I am given the problem, there are plenty of goals needed to fulfill when making various improvements on local ground including but not limited to the following:

  • Reducing steps for data mangement
  • Clean aesthetic
  • Accessible
  • Easy to manage data
  • All done on browser
